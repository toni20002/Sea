Being founded in the most progressive of centuries,
Séa Design manufactures all of its products using the most precise and efficient technologies.
Computer aided design and milling, metal 3D printing and laser engraving are a few of the technologies, that bring each concept to a complete product.
Séa’s precise manufacturing leaves minimal amount of unused material, that is smelted and reused again, thus minimising its environmental footprint.
Inspired by efficiency and simplicity, Séa is utilising modern and mindful solutions to achieve beauty.
